Clinic History


DKR Clinic was founded with a vision to address the growing incidence of heart diseases and lifestyle-related cardiac conditions. Recognizing the need for accessible and specialized heart care, the clinic began its journey as a trusted center for diagnosis and treatment of cardiovascular disorders. Over the years, DKR Clinic has earned the confidence of patients through quality care, accurate diagnosis, and consistent follow-up, becoming a reliable name in heart health services.

Our Mission


1. To provide high-quality, affordable, and ethical cardiac care
2. To promote preventive cardiology and heart-healthy lifestyles
3. To deliver patient-centered treatment with compassion and integrity
4. To use evidence-based medical practices and modern technology



Our Vision


1. To be a trusted and leading heart health clinic in the region
2. To reduce the burden of cardiovascular diseases through awareness and early intervention
3. To continuously improve healthcare standards through innovation and education
4. To build a healthier community with strong emphasis on prevention and long-term care
